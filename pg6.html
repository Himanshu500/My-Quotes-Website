Get the love for Music by these awe inspiring quotes from great people around the world!!!



1.   You must pass your days in a song,Let your whole life be a song.....
- Shri Sai Baba

2.   Music is like a dream. One that I cannot hear.....
- Ludwig van Beethoven

3.   Without music, life would be a mistake...
- Friedrich Nietzsche

4.   Music gives a soul to the universe, wings to the mind,
flight to the imagination and life to everything.....
- Plato

5.   How is it that music can, without words, evoke our laughter,
our fears, our highest aspirations?....
- Jane Swan

6.   One good thing about music, when it hits you, you feel no pain.....
- Bob Marley.

7.   The only truth is music....
- Jack Kerouac

8.   Music is the shorthand of emotion.....
- Leo Tolstoy.

9.   Without music, life would be a blank to me....
- Jane Austen.

10.   Music was my refuge. I could crawl into the
space between the notes and curl my back to loneliness.....
- Maya Angelou.
